Basic Settings

  1. In Font section change 'Font' to "Times New Roman"
  2. In Font section set 'Font Size' to "12"
  3. In Paragraph section set 'Line Spacing' to "2.0"

Formatting the Title Page

  1. In Paragraph section click "Align Text Left" button
  2. Type your name, press Enter
  3. Type your professor's name, press Enter
  4. Type the class name and section, press Enter
  5. Type "DD Month YYYY", press Enter

Create Header Template

  1. Click "Document Elements" in the Document menu
  2. Click "Header" and choose "Basic (All Pages)"
  3. Tap left "Type Text" to highlight and Delete
  4. Tap middle "Type Text" to highlight and Delete
  5. Tap right "Type Text" to highlight, type your last name
  6. In the "Document Elements" menu click "Page #"
  7. In Alignment dropdown menu of popup window choose "Right", click "OK"
  8. Click "Close" to close the Header and Footer sections
